About R.L. Anino Dental Clinic

Located in Tagum City, R.L. Anino Dental Clinic is committed to providing quality dental care with a focus on patient comfort, hygiene, and efficiency.

We understand that visiting the dentist can be stressful, which is why we’ve created a welcoming environment and a streamlined process to make your experience as smooth as possible.

Our clinic uses a modern, web-based management system to automate appointments, billing, and communication — reducing wait times and improving service accuracy.

  • No more double-booking
  • Secure online payments (GCash/PayPal)
  • Easy online booking (no login required)
  • Direct communication via inquiry form
